COVID Vaccination Data Exploration

This project aims to explore data surrounding COVID cases and vaccinations in the United States. Below, we have created a dashboard that will allow users to explore predicted outcomes based on changes to future vaccination rates. Users can investigate how the case rates of their chosen state will change over the coming months if their state changes their vaccination rate. There are additional visualizations under the 'Data Exploration' section that display information regarding state demographic information, the relationship between COVID cases and influenza-like illnesses, state policy decisions, and vaccine administration and distribution.
